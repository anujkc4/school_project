COW

The cow is a domestic animal known for its gentle nature and usefulness to humans. Cows are primarily kept for milk, which is a vital source of nutrition for people around the world. They also help in agriculture by producing manure that enhances soil fertility. In many cultures, cows hold a special religious and cultural significance.

buffalo

The buffalo is a strong and hardy animal known for its resilience and ability to thrive in various environments. It is commonly used for milk and as a draft animal in rural farming. Buffalo milk is thicker and more nutritious than cow's milk. They play an important role in agricultural labor and dairy industries in many countries.

Ox

The ox is a powerful animal mainly used for plowing fields and pulling carts in traditional farming. It is known for its endurance and strength, making it essential in agriculture before the advent of machines. Oxen are usually male cattle that have been trained to work, and they are highly valued in rural economies.
